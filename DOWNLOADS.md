### ‚ö° Quick Start (Windows)
1.  Download `yamlr.exe`.
2.  Move it to a folder (e.g., `C:\Program Files\Yamlr`).
3.  **Add that folder to your PATH environment variable.**
4.  Open a new terminal and type:
    ```powershell
    yamlr scan .
    ```
